Privacy Policy

If you choose to use CrookCatcher you automatically agree to this policy.

  • CrookCatcher asks users to log in with their gmail account and grant CrookCatcher access to 'sending emails'. CrookCatcher does this to avoid sending sensitive data (photos and location) over less reliable 3rd party servers.
  • CrookCatcher only uses this access to send Alert Emails, i.e. emails with a photo taken using the front facing camera and device's location.
  • Photos, Location as well as Alert Emails are only triggered when CrookCatcher has been activated by the user and receives a message from the android system that a wrong password has been entered.
  • Alert Emails are sent to the user's own email address (the same the sent the Alert Email) unless the option "Send email to..." in CrookCatcher's settings menu has been changed by the user.
  • We do not store nor have access user's email addresses.
  • We do store nor have access to the photos taken by CrookCatcher on users' devices.
  • We do store nor have access to locations fetched by CrookCatcher on users' devices.

No data is used for any other purpose than enabling CrookCatcher to function and make it better. No personal data is transmitted to any third party for advertising or any other purpose whatsoever.

CrookCatcher is in accordance with the Offical User Data Policy for Google API Services.

Log Data

CrookCatcher tracks anonymous usage data and crash reports using Google Analytics. The purpose of this is for the developers to find out how well CrookCatcher performs and improve it accordingly. You can turn this off by unticking the "Improve CrookCatcher" option in the app's settings menu.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
